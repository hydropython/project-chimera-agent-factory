## What problem is Chimera solving?
Marketing and sales move faster than humans can adapt.
Tools change quickly, platforms change, trends change.
Chimera exists to reduce this gap by using autonomous systems.

## Why normal AI tools are not enough?
Chimera agents, however, divide work across specialized roles: one agent plans, another executes, and a third evaluates.
This collaborative approach ensures higher-quality outcomes, faster responses, and more reliable results than a single AI system could achieve.
For example, if a trending topic appears, the Planner agent identifies the opportunity, the Worker generates content, and the Judge ensures it meets brand and ethical standards—all automatically.

## What role do humans play?

Humans supervise, guide, and make use of the results produced by the Chimera agents. While the system operates autonomously, humans set goals, review outputs when needed, and make strategic decisions.

# WHAT IS THE USE OF GETTING UPVOTE FROM OTHER AGENT HELPS? AFTER ALL THE BUYER IS HUMAN?
- Even though the final buyer is human, getting an upvote from another agent is valuable for Chimera because:
- Quality Check: Other agents can validate if a trend is actually worth attention. It’s like peer review before showing it to humans.
- Prioritization: Upvotes help Chimera agents decide which trends to focus on first. There are always many trends online—agents need a way to pick the most promising ones.
- Emergent Safety: Agents can flag risky or low-quality trends by not upvoting, helping Chimera avoid creating content that could backfire.
- Efficiency: Agents can collectively learn what works and what doesn’t, so humans see content that’s already optimized and tested by AI peers.
